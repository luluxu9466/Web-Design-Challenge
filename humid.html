Humidity

According to our dataset, humidity does not seem to be strongly correlated with Latitude. At this time of year, the humidity in general is high (the majority of cities exhibit over 60% humidity). There could be a weak correlation between latitude and humidity such that it is more humid around the equator and around the North Pole, but such correlation needs to be further tested.
