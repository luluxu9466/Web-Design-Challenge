Cloudiness

According to our dataset, there does not seem to be a correlation between cloudiness and latitude. However, there seems to be clustering around 100% cloudiness and 0% cloudiness, indicating that the cities are either not cloudy at all or completely cloudy at this time of year. This could be due to measurement criteria (definition of cloudiness).
