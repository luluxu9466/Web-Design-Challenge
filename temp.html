Max Temperature

The weather is significantly warmer as the latitude approaches the equator (Latitude is 0). More interestingly, however, the southern hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year compared to the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of earth at the time of the year this data was gathered. In this dataset, there are more data sampled in the north hemisphere compared to the south hemisphere.
